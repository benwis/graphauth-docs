Logs out of current user session by blacklisting their access and refresh tokens.
Does not require authentication because one must provide the access, refresh, or both tkens
to log them out.

```graphql
logout(
  tenantName: String!
  accessToken: String!
  refreshToken: String!
): Boolean!
```
